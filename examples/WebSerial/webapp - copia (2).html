Hardware Connection Guide

1. Identify the Battery Pins: Most laptop batteries use a standard SMBus interface. Click on any pin in the Live Connector Preview to select its function.

  • VCC / BAT+: Do NOT connect to ESP directly!
  • GND / BAT-: Connect to ESP GND
  • SCL (Clock): Connect to ESP D1
  • SDA (Data): Connect to ESP D2
  • SysPres (SP/T): Bridge to GND to wake up the battery.
Warning: Double check connections. Shorting VCC to data pins will damage your ESP!
